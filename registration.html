Registration for the 2021 MIT Science Olympiad Invitational Tournament will be open from September 12, 2020, 6 PM ET through September 20, 2020, 6 PM ET. Check back on this page for the registration form.

Quick Facts

  • Registration opens on September 12, 2020 at 6 PM ET
  • We are accepting up to 150 teams this season
  • Registration fee: $50
  • Please reach out to us at scioly@mit.edu if the registration fee prevents your team from participating

Timeline

  • September 12, 2020, 6 PM ET: Registration opens
  • September 20, 2020, 6 PM ET: Registration closes
  • September 28, 2020: Accepted teams notified and invited to register on Scilympiad
  • October 11, 2020: List of participating teams released

Acceptance Policy

For the 2021 season, we will be accepting up to 150 teams. If more than 150 teams register, schools meeting either of the following two conditions will be guaranteed admission:

  • Schools that have placed Top 20 at the 2020 MIT Invitational.
  • Schools that have placed Top 20 at the 2019 National Tournament.

All teams that do not meet the qualifications for guaranteed acceptance will have their acceptance determined by a lottery. Please note that registration does NOT follow a first-come-first-serve policy.

Registration is currently limited to one team per school but please indicate whether you would be interested in having a second team on the registration form.

Registration Fee

The registration fee for the 2021 MIT Invitational Tournament is $50, due by December 31, 2020. Please reach out to scioly@mit.edu if this fee and/or payment deadline prevents you from participating. You may pay via PayPal or by mailing a check to

Science Olympiad at MIT
P.O. Box 397105
Cambridge, MA 02139-7136

IMPORTANT: Note that the P.O. Box generally changes from year to year. If Science Olympiad at MIT has been entered as a vendor in your system, please update the address.
