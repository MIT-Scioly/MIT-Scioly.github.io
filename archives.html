2021 Invitational

The seventh annual MIT Science Olympiad Invitational Tournament was held on Friday, January 22nd, and Saturday, January 23rd, 2021 in our first ever virtual tournament. Congratulations to the champions, West Windsor-Plainsboro High School North from Plainsboro Township, New Jersey, and thank you to all coaches, event supervisors, volunteers, and students for making our seventh tournament a huge success!
