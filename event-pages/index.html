Protein Modeling

Protein Modeling will not be using the Mini Toober from 3D Molecular Designs. Instead, we will be using a foam wire tie substitute that can be found here. Amino acids will be indicated with tacks and termini will be indicated with colored tape. All other aspects of the on-site section will be standard.

Also, per national-level rules, each team may only bring ONE sheet of notes (double-sided) to the event.
