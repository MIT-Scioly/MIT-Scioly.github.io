Awards Ceremony

The awards ceremony will be held in Kresge Auditorium (W16-109) and will begin at 7:00 p.m. Doors open at 6:00 p.m.; please be seated no later than 6:55 p.m. so that the awards ceremony can start on time.

As is the procedure at the national tournament, due to space constraints, up to 16 people per team (usually, the 15 competing students plus 1 coach) may sit in the awards ceremony main venue (Kresge Auditorium, W16-109) for the awards ceremony. Additional tournament attendees may watch a live streaming video of the awards ceremony in the overflow seating location, in the same building.

Medals will be awarded to the top six teams in each event and trophies will be awarded to the top six schools at the tournament. Only the top-placing team from each school will be considered for a trophy.
