Acceptance Policy

For the 2021 season, we will be accepting up to 150 teams. If more than 150 teams register, schools meeting either of the following two conditions will be guaranteed admission:

  • Schools that have placed Top 20 at the 2020 MIT Invitational.
  • Schools that have placed Top 20 at the 2019 National Tournament.

All teams that do not meet the qualifications for guaranteed acceptance will have their acceptance determined by a lottery. Please note that registration does NOT follow a first-come-first-serve policy.

Registration is currently limited to one team per school but please indicate whether you would be interested in having a second team on the registration form.

Registration Fee

The registration fee for the 2021 MIT Invitational Tournament is $50 per team, due by December 31, 2020. Please reach out to scioly@mit.edu if this fee and/or payment deadline prevents you from participating. You may pay by mailing a check to
