Science Olympiad at MIT is an undergraduate student group founded in Fall 2013 by Connor Duffy (2017) and Kenny Friedman (2017) with the mission of improving the Science Olympiad experience of high school students in Massachusetts and the greater northeast region. MIT Science Olympiad is entirely run by a team of MIT undergraduate students. We work around the year both within MIT and in the Massachusetts area to promote Science Olympiad opportunities for students. The MIT Invitational would not be possible without the dedication and effort of our members.
