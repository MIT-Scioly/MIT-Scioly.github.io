Payment

For teams that have confirmed their attendance with us, you should have received an email with our W9 and an invoice. Please send a check addressed to MIT Science Olympiad for $130 per team to our PO box (PO 397105, Cambridge, MA 02139) by November 22nd, 2019. We will soon be in touch regarding the checks we have and have not received as well as invoices for individuals who need a formal receipt. If you have any questions or concerns, please email us at scioly@mit.edu.

Team Roster

Please submit a team roster by December 5th at this link. You will receive an email receipt to edit your submission since we recognize that team rosters may change over time. However, we are asking that that this form be submitted by December 5th, so we can set up self-scheduling using the Avogadro usernames (see below).

You will be able to keep track of which of your students have submitted what forms through a custom spreadsheet link that has been emailed to you. This sheet will be updated to reflect this information after December 5th.

Avogadro and Self-Scheduling

The roster form above asks for an Avogadro username. If you do not have an Avogadro account yet, please sign up for one on the Avogadro website.

Access to self-scheduled events will open on December 10th at 5PM EST and will be done through Avogadro. Another email with more details on self-scheduling will be sent closer to the date. Please be sure to fill out the roster form above by December 5 so that we can add your school to our Avogadro tournament.

MIT Liability Form

Please make sure the MIT liability form is submitted for each student (alternates included) attending the invitational by December 31st.

This form needs to be signed by a parent or a legal guardian. Note that when you first open the link, it asks for the name of the parent and not the student's name. Please follow this instruction since otherwise it will not work properly.

MIT Alert

All individuals coming onto MIT Campus must submit their information to receive emergency alerts in the case of a major incident. Please fill out the form here by December 31st. Parents, even for those who will not be on campus on the tournament day, can also sign up to receive alerts.

T-Shirts

If you would like to purchase 2020 MIT Invitational T-shirts, please use the form here. The design is shown in the form. Please indicate how many of each size you would like to order; payment is due on the day of the tournament in person. T-shirts will be $15 each. Please submit this form by December 31.
