Registration for the 2022 MIT Invitational Tournament will open on Wednesday, September 15th, 2021 at 6 PM ET and will close the following Wednesday, September 22nd at 6 PM ET.

Registration Form

Timeline

  • September 15, 2021, 6 PM ET: Registration opens
  • September 22, 2021, 6 PM ET: Registration closes
  • October 4, 2021: Accepted teams notified
  • October 11, 2021: List of participating teams released

Acceptance Policy

Schools meeting either of the following two conditions will be guaranteed admission:

  • Schools that have placed Top 10 at the MIT Invitational the previous year
  • Schools that have placed Top 10 at the National Tournament the previous year

All teams that do not meet the qualifications for guaranteed acceptance will have their acceptance determined by a lottery. Please note that registration does NOT follow a first-come-first-serve policy.
