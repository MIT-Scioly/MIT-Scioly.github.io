2018 Invitational

The fourth annual MIT Science Olympiad Invitational Tournament was held on Saturday, January 20, 2018, at the campus of MIT in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Congratulations to the champions, Troy High School from Fullerton, California, and thank you to all coaches, event supervisors, volunteers, and students for making our fourth tournament a huge success!
