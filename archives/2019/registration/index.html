Registration for the 2019 MIT Science Olympiad Invitational has closed! Thank you to all the teams that registered, and we look forward to hosting you in January.

Reserved spaces will be held pending payment of the registration fee of $130, due by November 22, 2018 . If a team has been placed on the waitlist, please do not pay the registration fee until you receive confirmation that your team has been accepted off the waitlist. Teams whose payment is not received by the deadline will forfeit their place to a team on the waitlist. Please contact us at scioly@mit.edu if meeting the payment deadline may pose a problem for your school.

Please make checks payable to Science Olympiad at MIT and mail to:

Science Olympiad at MIT
P.O. Box 397105
Cambridge, MA 02139-7136

IMPORTANT: Note that the P.O. Box generally changes from year to year. If Science Olympiad at MIT has been entered as a vendor in your system, please update the address.
