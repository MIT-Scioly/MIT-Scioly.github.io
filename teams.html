Day-Of Registration

Registration is mandatory for all teams—one coach from each school must come to registration. At registration, you will receive, per team:

  • 15 competitor wristbands
  • 0-4 alternate wristbands
  • 1 team list (includes homeroom)
  • 1 campus map
  • 1 parking map
  • 1 information brochure (explained below).
  • 1 schedule

We’ll also communicate any important day-of updates. We will also be using Remind 101 to communicate urgent updates with coaches day-of. At registration, we will have coaches sign up and confirm that the system works for them.

You can attend registration on either Friday, January 24 from 5-7pm or on Saturday, January 25 from 6-8am. You must attend registration during one of these times, but there is no need to go to both times. Registration will be held in HQ: 10-105, known as the Bush Room.
